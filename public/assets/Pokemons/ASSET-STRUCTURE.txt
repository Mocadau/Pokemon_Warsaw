Pokemon asset structure

Each Pokemon has its own folder:
`/public/assets/Pokemons/<Name>/`

Required structure for all new Pokemon:
- `<Name>.jpeg` (location photo with GPS metadata)
- `spawn<Name>.mp4`
- `throw<Name>.mp4`
- `pixel/<Name>.png`

After adding the files, extract the GPS coordinates from `<Name>.jpeg` and add
the finished Pokemon entry to `data/pokemonSpots.js` and
`public/assets/PokemonLocation/GPS-REFERENCE.json`.
